About

I am currently a graduate student in Computer Science at Stanford University, specializing in Artificial Intelligence. I am researching in the Stanford Vision Lab, advised by Professor Fei-Fei Li.

I earned my bachelor's degree in Electrical and Computer Engineering at University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign. During my undergraduate study, I worked in the Quantitative Light Imaging Laboratory with Professor Gabriel Popescu. I also did research in Computer Vision and Robotics Laboratory with Professor Narendra Ahuja and Dr. Jia-Bin Huang.
